## Welcome to TyphonJS-TCG

We provide tooling for various TCG / trading card games that are accessible for collectors and developers alike. Please come join us on Discord on the [![Discord](https://img.shields.io/discord/737953117999726592?label=TyphonJS&style=plastic)](https://typhonjs.io/discord/) 
server to chat about our TCG tooling. Look for the `tcg` / `#scrydex` channel and we look forward to chatting all things TCG / MTG.

[Scrydex](https://github.com/typhonjs-tcg/scrydex) is a CLI & SDK for final mile Magic The Gathering collection management.
